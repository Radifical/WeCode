What we do

Our goal at We Code is to provide a virtual community where people can learn about computer science, practice their coding skills, and test
themselves on their CS knowledge. We also provide services like creating and joining events, tutoring, and a question forum.


Our Services

We offer people around the world free tutoring in more than 5 languages in computer science. We also offer the opportunity to ask us
questions about your code so that we can help guide and debug your projects. Finally, we have multiple exclusive resources
like practice coding files and CS quizzes.
